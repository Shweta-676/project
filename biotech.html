What is Biotech?

Biotechnology is the use of living organisms, cells, and biological systems to create new products and technologies that improve human lives. It spans areas like genetic engineering, bioinformatics, and environmental sustainability.

Key Facts

  • Biotech is crucial for advancements in medicine, such as gene therapy and cancer treatment.
  • Biotechnology is used to develop sustainable agriculture solutions and renewable energy sources.
  • CRISPR gene editing is one of the most revolutionary tools in modern biotech.
  • By 2025, global biotech market revenues are projected to exceed $800 billion.
