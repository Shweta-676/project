Common Questions

Q: How is quantum computing different from classical computing?

A: Classical computers use bits (0s and 1s), while quantum computers use qubits, which can be both 0 and 1 simultaneously.

Q: What industries benefit from quantum computing?

A: Cryptography, drug discovery, finance, and artificial intelligence.
